# PMBOK question corrections

Updated on 24 June 2026.

## Scope

- Reviewed all 531 questions against PMBOK Guide Sixth Edition, PMBOK Guide Seventh Edition, and the Agile Practice Guide.
- Corrected 68 answer keys, incomplete questions, or damaged option sets.
- Added PMBOK-specific clarification to 46 ambiguous explanations.
- Restored damaged options from the original question screenshots.
- Added exact-set grading for questions that require two or three answers.

## Important corrections

- Integrated change control is required before changing approved scope or baselines.
- A risk that has occurred is managed as an issue in the issue log.
- CPI problems require cost analysis; crashing is a schedule-compression technique.
- Formal acceptance must come from the authorized stakeholder defined by governance documents.
- Product owners prioritize product value and backlog items but do not automatically have authority to cancel an entire project.
- Scrum of Scrums is used to coordinate multiple Scrum teams working on the same product.
- Tuckman's norming and performing stages are now distinguished correctly.
